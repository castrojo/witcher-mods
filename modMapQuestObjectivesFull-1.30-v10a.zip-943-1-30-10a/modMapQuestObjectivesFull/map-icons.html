CUSTOM ICONS

These icons can be used only in STANDARD and FULL versions. The LITE version does not include them!

While the red exclamation mark icons may look nice, they are not quite visible on world map. Especially if under icon is a building. The magenta and the pink seems to be more visible, but still not like the white, green, yellow or orange colors. Try them, maybe you have a better monitor or maybe I'm wrong.

The custom icons are rendered over any other icon, so they won't be hidden under some shopkeeper, notice board or monster den pin. This is not true for the vanilla icons, listed below in a separated table.

The exclamation mark icon was downloaded from http://www.clker.com/clipart-exclamation-3.html. According to their terms of use the icon should be in public domain.

Upgrade your browser if you don't see the icons.
